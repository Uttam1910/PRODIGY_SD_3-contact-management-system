Welcome to the Contact Management System

Our system provides a comprehensive solution to manage, organize, and maintain all your important contacts. Whether you're storing business contacts or personal connections, our easy-to-use platform ensures efficiency and organization at every step.

Key Features

  • Add New Contacts: Quickly add new contacts with essential details like name, email, phone number, and more.
  • View Contact List: Easily browse your contact list with detailed information displayed in a table format.
  • Edit/Delete Contacts: Make changes to your contact details or remove them as necessary.
  • Recycle Bin: Restore contacts that were previously soft-deleted from the recycle bin.
  • Search & Filter: Use the search function to filter through your contacts and find what you need instantly.
  • Mobile Friendly: Access your contacts on any device with our responsive design.

How It Works

Step 1: Add a Contact

Navigate to the 'Add Contact' page where you can enter and save essential contact details like name, phone number, and email address.

Step 2: View and Edit Contacts

Visit the 'Contacts List' page to view all saved contacts. Click on a contact to edit or update their information as needed.

Step 3: Manage the Recycle Bin

Deleted a contact by mistake? Go to the 'Recycle Bin' to restore contacts you've accidentally removed or permanently delete them.
